What is EcoTrack?

EcoTrack is a community-driven platform designed to help individuals and organizations take meaningful environmental actions. Whether you are a student, an activist, a volunteer group, or a professional environmental organization, EcoTrack provides the tools to track, share, and manage sustainable actions and eco-friendly events.

For Users

  • Create and share sustainable actions.
  • Join eco-events hosted by local groups or organizations.
  • Earn points for participating in events and completing actions.
  • Redeem points for rewards from the Rewards page.
  • Explore new ideas and trends through the Explore page.

For Organizers

  • Create events for cleanups, drives, sustainability workshops, and more.
  • Promote your mission to the community through posts and updates.
  • Engage with participants through actions and event sign-ups.
  • Track participation engagement through posted content.

EcoTrack brings the community together to create real environmental impact, one action at a time.
